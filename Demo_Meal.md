## 1. Executive Summary (The "Elevator Pitch")

### The Problem: The "Dinner Party" Dilemma
Imagine you are hosting a dinner party for 300 strangers. You don't know what they like to eat, so you just guess: *"Let's cook 50% Chicken and 50% Beef."*
**The Result?**
1.  You have 50 leftover Chicken meals (Waste).
2.  You run out of Beef, and 50 guests go hungry or angry (Bad Experience).

Now, imagine doing this for **200 flights every single day**. That is our current situation. We rely on "historical averages" and "guessing."

### The Solution
Our **AI Meal Prediction System** acts like a smart event planner. It looks at the guest list (Passenger Manifest), checks the calendar (Is it Friday?), and understands the destination culture. It tells us *exactly* what to load.

### Why We Need This Specifically
1.  **Stop Waste:** We currently over-load meals "just in case." This tool cuts that cost immediately.
2.  **Happy Passengers:** We ensure the right meal is available for the right person. No more *"Sorry, we are out of your choice."*
3.  **Speed:** A human takes hours to analyze a flight manifest. This AI does it in 20 seconds.
